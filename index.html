Our Daily Rhythm

A balanced day of learning, play & growth

8:00 – 8:15 AM

Breakfast Together

We start our day with a healthy breakfast, good conversation, and gratitude.

8:15 – 8:45 AM

Arrival & Morning Invitations

Children are welcomed with warm greetings, free play, sensory activities, books, and engaging learning invitations.

8:45 – 9:15 AM

Morning Meeting

We gather for songs, calendar, weather, ASL signs, and our daily focus.

9:15 – 10:00 AM

Literacy Workshop

Stories, letters, phonics, writing, and language development through hands-on exploration.

10:00 – 10:45 AM

Discovery Lab

STEM experiments, nature studies, sensory play, and problem-solving fun.

10:45 – 11:15 AM

Snack & Conversation

Healthy snack time and meaningful conversation together.

11:15 AM – 12:00 PM

Outdoor Exploration

Fresh air, gross motor play, nature walks, gardening, and outdoor discovery.

12:00 – 12:30 PM

Creative Studio

Art, music, movement, and imaginative creative expression.

12:30 – 1:00 PM

Lunch

A nourishing lunch together, followed by helping clean up and set the table.

1:00 – 3:00 PM

Rest & Quiet Time

Rest, nap, or quiet activities to recharge our bodies and minds.

3:00 – 3:30 PM

Afternoon Discovery

Small group learning, fine motor activities, puzzles, and engaging centers.

3:30 – 4:00 PM

Music & Movement

Songs, dances, instruments, and movement to build joy and confidence.

4:00 – 4:45 PM

Open Play — Indoor or Outdoor

Children choose play experiences they love as they prepare for pickup.

4:45 – 5:00 PM

Goodbyes & Sweet Endings

We close out the day with pickup, hugs, and happy hearts!
